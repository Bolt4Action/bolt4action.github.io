Internalization via Externalization: ([[Writing is thinking, The Power of Externalization]])

  • When you consume knowledge, It can either:
    • Get loaded into your short-term memory, Forgetting it soon after.
    • Get loaded into your long-term memory, But in an unorganized way.
    • Get loaded in your long-term memory in an organized way.
  • One benefit of Externalization is that when you write your ideas in concrete form, That helps you

  • In order to offload by internalization, You have to consciously repeat the action until it is internalized in a habit, Or consciously grapple with a thought long enough that it becomes intuitive (Ex: Problem-Solving, Decision-Making and Language Learning).

  • Some of your Intuition is so deeply rooted, That it requires External Hooks & Pointers so you can Externalize this knowledge and deal with it in concrete form, Organizing it then it can be internalized again.
